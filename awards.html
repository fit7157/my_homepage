I'm Seung Min Kim. I'm in 4th year of my university now. I'm in AI robotics major, Sejong university.
I was not in AI robotics major from the first place. I enrolled in Sejong university in 2020 and my major was civil and environmental engineering, which learns about physics for building social infrastructure like bridges,dams,road, etc..
I really enjoyed learning there. However, I wanted to learn about robotics and cars. I wanted to study vehicles, UAV, robots and other moving things. I wanted to learn mechatronics that is used for engineering. Luckly I got a chance to change my major, therefore, I changed my major to AI robotics.
At first I had really hard time studying in this major. I have studied physics and math but in AI robotics I needed coding skill. While going to school, I got help from coding academy for many months. thankfully I could get A in the second year after moving.
Then I started to get interest in robots, which I really wanted to study. Thankfully, I learned Arduino and HTML in school. I started to make my own robot using simple codes and metarials. ATOV is my first wheel and leg based robot and it is biproduct of my project.
After making my robots, I made my homepage using HTML in order to introduce my robots. I showed it to professors. One of them, professor Muhammad Zubair was interested in my robot projects. He told me he can help me build robots more. I met him regularly while studying courses. Studying courses and making robots were two things that I focused on most.
I realized that AI is crucial for robots. I became an Intership student in professor Zubairs' lab. I started learning AI.
