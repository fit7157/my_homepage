My understanding

Rail road detection is one of the field that needs to transfer man power based detection to AI based detection.
In this process we should discuss how we can improve computre vision in order to achieve AI algorithm.
This paper suggests lots of ways regarding rail road field.

This picture shows all methods. Satellite, drones, trains, way-side cameras,trolley can be used. Each methods have positive effects and challenges.
1. Satellites can easily recognize big issues. Forexample when a bridge starts to sink, which can be a signal of collapse, satellite can notice it by comparing two images taken in different time.
2. Drones are efficient in surveillence railroads. However short battery life is a chellenge.
3. Train borne cameras are also good way. Cameras that are mounted under train can detect railroad crack or railroad defects. While cameras that are mounted on the roof can detect over head lines, pantograph and catenary. THis is effiecient because it can easily detect long rail roads.
4. way-side cameras can detect cracks on railroads. It needs to be installed firmly in order to endure vibrations.
5. Trolley borne camera is final way to detect railroads. It can detect entird railroads in a low cost. However unlike train borne method it needs special scheduling for operation.
